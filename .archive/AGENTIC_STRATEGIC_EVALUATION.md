## âš ï¸ RISKS & MITIGATION

### Critical Risks

**1. Legal/Regulatory Risk**
- **Risk:** Psychedelic therapy is Schedule I federally; legal landscape uncertain
- **Mitigation:**
  - Focus on education and research, not illegal activity
  - Clear disclaimers and terms of service
  - Legal review of all clinical features
  - Geo-restrict features based on jurisdiction

**2. Safety & Liability Risk**
- **Risk:** Platform users may experience mental health crises or adverse events
- **Mitigation:**
  - Robust crisis resources and professional partnerships
  - Clear disclaimers that platform is not medical advice
  - Terms of service limit liability
  - Professional liability insurance
  - Strict content moderation for harm reduction

**3. Privacy & HIPAA Compliance**
- **Risk:** Clinical case discussions may involve PHI (Protected Health Information)
- **Mitigation:**
  - All case discussions must be anonymized
  - End-to-end encryption for sensitive discussions
  - HIPAA compliance audit and certification
  - User training on privacy best practices
  - Regular security audits

**4. Cultural Appropriation Risk**
- **Risk:** Platform may inadvertently appropriate indigenous practices
- **Mitigation:**
  - Authentic partnerships with indigenous communities
  - Revenue sharing and reciprocity projects
  - Content review by cultural advisors
  - Ongoing education on decolonization
  - Platform for indigenous voices, not about them

**5. Misinformation Risk**
- **Risk:** Platform could spread dangerous misinformation about psychedelics
- **Mitigation:**
  - AI content moderation (Phase 8 GenAI feature)
  - Expert review of educational content
  - Citation requirements for medical claims
  - Clear fact vs. opinion labeling
  - Community reporting and moderation
